    How it works

    1. Enter a topic, number of slides, and choose a theme.
    2. Click Generate Outline to auto-create slide titles & bullets.
    3. Click any slide to edit its content. Add or remove slides as needed.
    4. Click Download PPTX to get a PowerPoint file you can open in MS PowerPoint, Keynote, or Google Slides.

    Optional: Integrate your own AI API endpoint in app.js (see README) to generate richer content.
